DiceMannor respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the DiceMannor iPad app collects and how it is used.

DiceMannor does not collect personal information.

DiceMannor stores gameplay progress locally on your device so you can continue your run later. This data stays on your device unless you remove the app or your device operating system provides separate backup behavior.

Network Use

DiceMannor is designed to be playable offline and does not require a network connection for normal gameplay.
